Together We Can

Pankh Foundation is an Govt Registered NGO with more than 5,000 active volunteers in india based in Kolkata (WB). We are an organization that helps the underprivileged and weaker Sections of the Society in all the possible ways such as Education, Healthcare, Foodings and many more. We organize free Health and Eye check Camps every year supporting and providing more than 5000 free optical to the poor and needy. We ensure that every child goes to school in whatever situation they are in. Our active volunteers from 50 different college visits more than 500 underprivilege in various slum every week teaching them the basic manners ,importance of Education and importance of staying clean . Our aim is to create an inclusive society, where people who are underprivilege and weak are accepted into the mainstream economy and social life and gets an opportunity to excel in their life ahead.
